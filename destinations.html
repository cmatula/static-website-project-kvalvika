Northern Norway

Experience Northern Norway with the midnight sun, the northern lights, and endless hiking and nature. You will see mountain peaks overlooking white beaches and stunning scenery wherever you look.

Lofoten

Wild and rugged peaks. Arctic blue waves breaking onto white sandy beaches. There is no wonder the islands of Lofoten has gained a worldwide reputation for tourists. The archipelago is of Norway’s most visited and treasured destinations and offer a wide range of activities as well as stunning sights.

Reine

A fishing village located on the island of Moskenesøya in the Lofoten archipelago. Here you will see a picturesque small village lined with red rorburers (fisherman's houses).

Svolvaer

The picturesque harbour of Svolvær overlooks the Vestfjord. The surrounding islands, steep mountains, beautiful beaches and sheltered bays offer opportunities to take part in a number of exciting activities. Shops, galleries, cafes and restaurants are scattered throughout the city.

Senja

Senja is a jewel on the North-Norwegian coast which boasts staggering peaks and magnificent fjords. The island is Norway’s second largest and one you won't want to miss!

Tromso

Experiencing the beautiful midnight sun should be on everyone's bucket list and it is something well worth visiting Tromsø for. You can hike under the midnight sun in summer or see the northern lights in winter. It’s famed as a viewing point for colourful Northern Lights that sometime light up the nighttime sky.
