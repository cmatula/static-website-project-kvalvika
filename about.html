About Us

We are an Australian & Polish couple with a love of nature and hiking - especially in one of the most beautiful places in the world to hike - the Lofoten Islands. We want to share our passion of travel and show you the magic of Northern Norway.

Travel is second nature to us, having lived in Norway and travelled extensively in the Lofoten Islands. Your guide will be able to offer first-hand knowledge about hiking and adventure and will make sure you have the holiday of your dreams with unforgettable experiences.
